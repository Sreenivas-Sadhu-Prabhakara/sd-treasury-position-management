# run this service domain standalone

helm upgrade --install sd-treasury-position-management ./helm -n bian-operations

FAQQuestions

What is the BIAN “Manage” functional pattern?
A Manage service domain plans, coordinates and oversees a resource or activity across its lifecycle. Treasury Position Management applies it to the Treasury Position.
What is a BIAN service domain?
BIAN (the Banking Industry Architecture Network) decomposes a bank into independent service domains, each owning exactly one business capability. Treasury Position Management is the service domain for Markets and Treasury; it manages the Treasury Position Management Plan and exposes it through standard BIAN action terms.
What business area does Treasury Position Management belong to?
Treasury Position Management sits in the Operations and Execution business area — the operational core that executes payments, positions, settlements and transactions. It runs in the bian-operations namespace as one independent Spring Boot microservice.
Is Treasury Position Management production-ready?
It is an educational reference implementation: a real BIAN REST API over an in-memory store, ready to be filled with domain logic. It is not a live bank and is not affiliated with BIAN.
